WRITING SAMPLES




"An Analysis of the Relationship Between Eviction Rates and Upward Mobility"

Written in May 2019 as part of "Empirical Research in Income Inequality," an independent research course, which uses a fixed-effects model to assess the causal relationship between eviction rates and upward intergenerational mobility.


"Obesity, Food Deserts, and Minorities: A Case Study of Colorado"

Written in April 2018 as part of "Big Data in Economics," my sophomore introduction to applied econometric techniques and big data concepts.


"Measuring the Impact of Class Size on Student Outcomes"

Empirical project as part of Professor Raj Chetty's "Using Big Data to Solve Economic Problems" in which the class replicated a popular 2017 paper that used a regression discontinuity design to estimate causal effect of class size on student outcomes. All the tables and figures produced solely in R (stargazer, rdd, etc.), and final .pdf output created with R Markdown.
